Flipping card with JavaScript onclick

The scene on the left has a card in it, with two sides.

Things are laid out with grid, just to center things, but the 2 sides are positioned aboslute to put them in the same place.

The scene gets the perspective, the card needs preserve-3d, and the sides need their backface-visibility hidden.

The back side get rotated on the Y axis 180deg.

There's a .flipped class waiting for javascript to apply it when the card is clicked on.

I replaced the fixed pixel dimensions to make the whole thing responsive.
